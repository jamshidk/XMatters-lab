About Xmatters Lab

Engineering Confidence from Blueprint to Certification

Xmatters Lab brings decades of hands-on expertise in aerospace manufacturing processes and structural integrity assessment. We partner with OEMs, Tier-1 suppliers, and MRO operators to ensure every component performs flawlessly under the most demanding conditions.

Our team of certified engineers combines advanced simulation tools with deep materials knowledge to deliver analysis and consulting that is both rigorous and actionable.

  • FAA/EASA regulatory compliance consulting
  • FEA & fatigue life analysis for primary structures
  • Manufacturing process optimization & DFM review
  • Composites, titanium, and high-strength alloy expertise
  • Full lifecycle support from prototype to production
What We Do

Core Engineering Services

From first-principles analysis to full manufacturing support, we cover the complete technical lifecycle of aerospace components.

Structural Analysis & FEA

Static, dynamic, and fatigue analysis of airframe and propulsion components using industry-validated FEA methodologies and software.

Manufacturing Process Consulting

DFM reviews, process capability studies, tooling design guidance, and production readiness assessments for aerospace-grade component fabrication.

Fatigue & Damage Tolerance

Crack growth analysis, safe-life and damage-tolerance assessments per FAA AC 25.571, ensuring airworthiness throughout the component lifecycle.

03
Composites Engineering

Layup design, coupon testing review, delamination analysis, and repair scheme development for CFRP, GFRP, and hybrid composite structures.

04
Regulatory & Certification Support

Compliance documentation, DER coordination, and certification planning support for FAA, EASA, and international airworthiness authorities.

05
Material Selection & Testing

Material characterization, supplier qualification, coupon and coupon-level test witnessing, and material allowables generation for aerospace applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

We analyze a wide range of primary and secondary structures including airframe panels, wing spars, fuselage frames, nacelles, control surfaces, landing gear components, engine mounts, and interior structural elements. We work across commercial, business aviation, defense, and space applications.

Our team works with industry-standard tools including NASTRAN, ABAQUS, ANSYS, and HyperMesh. We operate under FAA Advisory Circulars, MIL-HDBK-5/MMPDS, CMH-17 for composites, and relevant ASTM and SAE standards to ensure full regulatory alignment.
